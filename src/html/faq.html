Frequently Asked Questions

"I can't find any books in the library, where are they?""

Unfortunately, due to legal reasons, we are not able to directly provide users with a digital library of books already present.

"Where can I get the files needed to use your website?"

Finding your favorite books in epub format is easily done through a quick use of your preferred search engine. Afterwards, when you finally have the file you want to read, just upload it to our site and off you go!

"Is there a catch to using this website?"

The only "catch" would be that you have to provide your own epub files. Other than that, using this site is safe and free for public use. Happy reading!
